Grand Mosque of West Sumatera

The largest mosque in West Sumatra, and the second largest mosque in Sumatra. Standing on 40,343 square meter on the intersection of Jalan Khatib Sulaiman and Jalan Ahmad Dahlan.

Malin Kundang Beach

The existence of Batu Malin Kundang has popularized Air Manis Beach, the legendary setting as one of the tourist attractions in Padang. The relief on the Malin Kundang stone itself was done in the 1980s, the work of Dasril Bayras with Ibenzani Usman.

Siti Nurbaya Bridge

TThe name is Siti Nurbaya Bridge, which is located in South Padang Sub-District. The bridge resides at Nipah Street and belongs to Batang Arau Village, actually. The name derives from a famous classic novel and the bridge connects Seberang Padang with the city center.
